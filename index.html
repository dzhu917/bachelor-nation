In each season, only one contestant can be crowned a winner (i.e. get engaged to the Bachelor(ette). The show usually depicts the Bachelor(ette)'s choice between the final two contestants as heart-wrenching and nearly impossible, because of how deep both relationships have become.

If the runner-ups and winners are really so difficult to choose between, how do their paths differ from each other? Is there any way to predict who will win and who will come in second place?

For each season, we're focusing only on winners and runner-ups: winner on top, runner-up on bottom.
