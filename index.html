Bachelor Nation

Each contestants is represented as a dot. The blue ones represent Bachelor contestants, and the orange ones represent Bachelorette contestants. Each cluster contains one season of contestants. Toggle over each contestant to find out more about them!

Winners in each season (i.e. the contestant selected by the Bachelor/Bachelorette at the end of the season are differentiated by size.

Different colors in each cluster indicate elimination week. The lighter the color of the dot, the earlier the contestant was eliminated. Winners, therefore, are represented by the darkest dot in each cluster.

Contestants are sorted based on elimination week. During each season, contestants are eliminated week by week until only one contestant remains. Typically, this winning contestant gets engaged to the Bachelor/Bachelorette.

First impression roses as an indicator of success

In the first week of each season, one contestant received a "First Impression Rose".

Out of all Bachelor and Bachelorette seasons, 26 first impression roses were given out. Of the contestants who received a first impression rose, 7 (27%) of them would go on to win the show. In contrast, only 26 out of the 860 (3%) other contestants that have been on the show ultimately won their season
