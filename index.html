Bachelor Nation

All contestants are represented as dots. Teal ones are Bachelor contestants. Orange ones are Bachelorette contestants. Separated by season. Winners are differentiated by size in the following visualization.

First impression roses as an indicator of success

In the first week of each season, one contestant received a "First Impression Rose".

Out of all Bachelor and Bachelorette seasons, 26 first impression roses were given out. Of the contestants who received a first impression rose, 7 (27%) of them would go on to win the show. In contrast, only 26 out of the 860 (3%) other contestants that have been on the show ultimately won their season.
